Who I am
and what I do 我是誰
以及我的專長

I am Abdiwahab M. Ali, a doctoral student at National Yang Ming Chiao Tung University (NYCU), Taipei, Taiwan. My research focuses on global cancer epidemiology, obesity and non-communicable disease trends, and burden-of-disease analysis. I have worked as a data analyst with hospitals and cancer centers in Taiwan. 我是國立陽明交通大學（NYCU）的博士生，研究聚焦於全球癌症流行病學、肥胖與非傳染性疾病趨勢，以及疾病負擔分析。我曾在台灣多家醫院及癌症中心擔任資料分析師。

I handle datasets from GBD, GLOBOCAN, NCD Risk Factor Collaboration, and WHO indicators. I have processed a single dataset with over 18 million rows. Analysis is done in R, Stata, and Joinpoint software. I also support manuscript drafting and can work in SPSS for clients who need it. 我處理來自GBD、GLOBOCAN、NCD Risk Factor Collaboration及WHO指標的資料集，曾處理單一超過1,800萬筆的資料集。分析使用R、Stata及Joinpoint軟體，同時提供論文撰寫支援，也可配合客戶需求使用SPSS。

Services服務項目

What I offer 服務內容

01
Statistical Analysis統計分析
Regression (linear, logistic, Poisson), joinpoint regression, time-series, survival analysis, mixed models. I work in R, Stata, and Joinpoint depending on what the method requires. 迴歸分析（線性、邏輯斯、卜瓦松）、接合點迴歸、時間序列、存活分析、混合模型。依方法需求使用R、Stata或Joinpoint。
02
Figure Production (R)圖表製作（R）
Choropleth maps, heatmaps, spaghetti plots, dumbbell charts, lollipop plots, scatter composites, stacked bars, faceted panels. Built in ggplot2, sf, and tmap to journal specifications. 等值地圖、熱圖、折線圖、啞鈴圖、棒棒糖圖、散點複合圖、堆疊長條圖、分面圖。以ggplot2、sf及tmap依期刊規格製作。
03
Epidemiological Analysis流行病學分析
Global burden of disease, AAPC computation, age-period-cohort analysis, SDI stratification, sex-disaggregated comparisons, cross-country trend analysis using GBD, GLOBOCAN, and WHO datasets. 全球疾病負擔、AAPC計算、年齡-時期-世代分析、SDI分層、性別分層比較、使用GBD、GLOBOCAN及WHO資料的跨國趨勢分析。
04
Large Dataset Processing大型資料集處理
Data cleaning, restructuring, and analysis at scale. Familiar with GBD, GLOBOCAN, NCD-RisC, UN WPP, and WHO indicator formats. Handled a single dataset with over 18 million rows. 大規模資料清理、重組與分析。熟悉GBD、GLOBOCAN、NCD-RisC、聯合國WPP及WHO指標格式，曾處理超過1,800萬筆的單一資料集。
05
Manuscript Support論文撰寫支援
Methods sections, results drafting, statistical reporting, table formatting, and figure preparation for journal submission. I can also help with revision responses. 方法學撰寫、結果陳述、統計報告、表格排版及圖形準備，協助投稿期刊。也可協助回應審查意見。
06
Reports and Presentations報告與簡報
Slide decks for conferences, clinical summaries, policy briefs, and reports with embedded figures. Can produce in R Markdown, PowerPoint, or HTML format. 學術會議簡報、臨床摘要、政策報告及嵌入圖表的研究報告。可用R Markdown、PowerPoint或HTML格式產出。
